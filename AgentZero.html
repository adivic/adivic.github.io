Agent Zero

2021

Third-Person single-player game. Created in Unreal Engine 4. The logic was achieved using the blueprints system, such as objective logic, guards logic, and overall gameplay. The map is planned as an abandoned city filled with radiation. The main objective is to sneak in and grab the intel from the guarded territory. Easter egg is also incorporated into the game as a story.
